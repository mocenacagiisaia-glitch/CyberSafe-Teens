Cyber Safety Tips

Small habits make a big difference. Follow these key rules.

Don't share OTP

OTP is private, never share it with anyone.

Avoid suspicious downloads

Only install apps from trusted stores and verified sources.

Use two-factor authentication

Enable 2FA to add a second layer of protection to accounts.

Avoid public Wi-Fi for banking

Public Wi-Fi is unsafe for sensitive account access without a VPN.
